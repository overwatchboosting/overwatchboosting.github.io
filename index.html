Overwatch Competitive Boosting

Overwatch boosting is for those who would like to quickly and painlessly raise their skill rating (SR) in the Overwatch competitive format. Whether you want to escape the frustrating climb out of ELO hell, earn better end of season rewards, or rank up so you can group up with higher ranked friends, we can help.

We are a group of North American Overwatch players who started off by boosting friends and friends of friends as favors during Season 2. Our boosters are Season 3 Grandmasters and have been ranked in the top 1% of players by Master Overwatch.

How it Works

  Solo Boosts

Solo boosts are how boosting is traditionally done. A solo boost involves a high skill player playing directly on your account and as a result, costs less than a group boost. For account security, a temporary password should be set on your Battle.net account for the duration of the boost. Additionally, if you have a smart phone, it's recommended to enable the Battle.net mobile authenticator for additional peace of mind.

  Group Boosts

Group boosts involve you playing on your account alongside our boosters. Our boosters are flexible and will work around your schedule and availability. While the group boost is occurring you may request coaching to help improve your gameplay or to understand the dynamics of the matches you've been playing in.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why are solo boosts cheaper than group boosts?
In order to perform a group boost it's necessary to purchase and prepare alternate accounts for our boosters to play on. The extra time required to get these accounts to the appropriate SR to group up often takes longer than the boost itself. On the other hand, none of this is necessary when performing solo boosts.

Q: How long does it normally take?
Boost duration is mostly dependent on your starting and desired SR. Solo boosts tend to be faster due to the flexibility in scheduling and can often be completed within a day, sometimes two. Group boosts largely depend on your availability. If you are available to play for many hours a day then the boost duration will be similar to that of solo boost.

Q: Will you play specific heroes?
This is a possibility largely dependent on desired SR. Please get in touch using the contact form on the right to discuss this possibility.

Q: Do you offer coaching?
While we are willing to offer advice and tips during group boosts, we don't consider this the core service that we are offering. If you are interested in receiving dedicated coaching, we recommend the following discord group which does have volunteer coaching: https://discord.gg/VxYhChh.

Q: Do you offer grandmaster (GM) boosts?
Yes. Grandmaster boosts are available but only if done as a solo boost. Please use the form on the right to contact us in order to discuss it.

Q: Do you offer refunds?
Yes. If a boost is not fulfilled within a reasonable timeframe for any reason we will completely refund it.

Q: How can I trust you?
We have successfully performed boosts for many players. We do this in our spare time outside of our regular lives where we work and study. If you are at all uncertain about our authenticity then please reach out with your concerns using the contact form on the right! We can play a few games with you before any purchase is made so that you can see we are not only genuine but also capable.
